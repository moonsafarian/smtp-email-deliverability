# Popular Smart Host Services and Their Company Locations

Choosing the right smart host is crucial for ensuring optimal email deliverability. Different providers offer varying features, pricing models, and regional focuses. Below is a list of some popular smart host services and their primary company headquarters locations. This can be helpful for understanding potential data residency implications or regional support.

*   **SendGrid:** Denver, Colorado, USA
*   **Mailgun:** San Antonio, Texas, USA
*   **Postmark:** Indianapolis, Indiana, USA
*   **SparkPost (MessageBird):** Amsterdam, Netherlands (with offices globally, including the USA)
*   **Amazon SES (Simple Email Service):** Seattle, Washington, USA (Service is highly distributed globally)
*   **SMTP.com:** New York, New York, USA
*   **Elastic Email:** Vancouver, Canada
*   **Mailjet (Sinch):** Paris, France and London, UK (Sinch is headquartered in Sweden)
*   **SMTP2GO:** Auckland, New Zealand
*   **TurboSMTP:** Milan, Italy
*   **SocketLabs:** Boston, Massachusetts, USA
*   **Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) SMTP Relay:** Mountain View, California, USA (Service is highly distributed globally)

This list is not exhaustive, but provides a good starting point for exploring smart host options based on your needs, including geographical considerations.
